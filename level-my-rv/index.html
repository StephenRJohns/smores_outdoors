How to use it
  1. Park in the spot you actually want, engine off, parking brake on (and transmission in Park or in gear).
  2. Stick a couple of cheap bar/bubble levels on a flat rear-facing surface and a flat side surface — or just hold a phone level app in place. Drag the matching bubble above to the same spot; it floats toward the HIGH side, so the low side is the opposite.
  3. Lay the block stacks just ahead of the low tires, drive up slowly, chock a wheel.
  4. Re-check the levels. Suspension squish and stack nesting mean one refinement round is normal.
