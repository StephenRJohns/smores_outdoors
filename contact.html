Suggesting vehicle data

The RV Leveling Block Calculator has a built-in form for suggesting a vehicle for its lookup database — it pre-fills the measurements you entered, so it's the fastest way to send us a rig we're missing. For corrections to a vehicle already in the database, plain email works great: include the year, make, model, configuration, and the source for your numbers.
